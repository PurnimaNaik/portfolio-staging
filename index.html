About

I have previously worked as a Front-End developer in an agile environment, delivering 5 applications under 6 months for the R&D department of a retail business chain in the U.S. I have also worked as an iOS developer for an enterprise management software company, updating and maintaining their proprietary mobile application using Objective-C.

Aside from traditional software development roles, I have worked as a Digital Marketing SEO Analyst for over a year. I am proficient in identifying trends to provide quantitive business building propositions that improve rankings, leads, and conversions. On my own time, I have created e-commerce websites, native & hybrid mobile apps, designed databases, and created models using machine learning algorithms.

On a side note, organizing a project is a strong asset of mine. Recent projects
CropReco Mobile App
The app aims to reduce or completely eradicate the number of farmers committing suicides in rural India; farmers loan money to buy farming equipment and are debt-ridden if healthy crops dont yield on time. Developed a native iOS app that recommends crops based on the climatic conditions in order to produce maximum yield. Users signup to receive crop recommendations based on temperature, humidity, sunlight received, and preferred yield period. Admin adds, deletes, and updates the crop details; also manages user profiles.

Wall Street Journal Mobile Website

Integrated cookies and hidden form field to track user clicks and show customized content.
